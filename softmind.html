Mental Health • Emotional UX

SoftMind

A mental health experience designed to reduce anxiety by removing pressure, metrics, and decision overload.

Problem

Most mental health apps overwhelm users with dashboards, streaks, and excessive tracking. During anxiety, users need safety — not performance.

User

Adam, 30 — office employee. Experiences anxiety spikes and avoids complex interfaces.

Design Approach

  • One emotional state per screen
  • No numbers, no dashboards
  • Supportive microcopy
  • Slow, predictable flows

User Flow

Open app → emotional check-in → guided breathing → reassurance → exit without fatigue

Key Insight

UX can act as emotional regulation. When users feel safe, engagement becomes natural.
