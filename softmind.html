SoftMind

Mental health tracking app designed for emotional awareness and stress reduction.

View Figma Prototype

Project Overview

SoftMind is a non-clinical mental health tracking app to help users understand emotional patterns, reduce daily stress, and build emotional awareness through gentle interactions. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Problem Statement

Many mental health apps overwhelm users with information, complex dashboards, and excessive tracking. Users experiencing anxiety need simplicity, reassurance, and emotional safety.

User Persona

Adam — Age 30
Office employee with daily anxiety spikes and decision fatigue.

UX Objective

Reduce decision fatigue and emotional friction during app usage through simple, reassuring interactions.

UX Strategy

  • Calm color palette & soft contrast
  • Minimal text per screen
  • Clear emotional states mapping
  • Predictable navigation patterns

User Flow

1. User opens app during stress
2. Immediate emotional check-in
3. Guided calming action
4. Positive reinforcement
5. Exit without cognitive fatigue

Design Decisions

  • Soft calm palette to avoid overstimulation
  • Rounded shapes to evoke safety
  • Short microcopy + supportive text
  • No dashboards or stress-increasing features

Results & Key Learnings

SoftMind concept demonstrates lower emotional friction and higher comfort through simple flows. Focus on emotional state to drive interface decisions — UX can act as emotional regulation.
