Paper & Soul

An editorial e-commerce experience designed for calm, mindful browsing.

Problem Statement

Most e-commerce platforms prioritize conversion over experience, resulting in cluttered interfaces that overwhelm users and disconnect them emotionally from products.

User Persona

Lina, 28 — reader & creative professional. Values aesthetics, calm environments, and meaningful discovery.

UX Strategy

  • Editorial layout inspired by magazines
  • Generous white space
  • Reduced promotional noise
  • Focus on typography & rhythm

User Flow

Homepage → Editorial selection → Product immersion → Simple checkout → Post-purchase satisfaction

Selected screens exported from Figma

Key Learnings

Calm, editorial UX increases emotional connection. Reducing visual noise improves browsing time and brand perception.
